Lila is a 27-year-old communication expert, responsible for maintaining the fragile links between Mars and any remaining Earth stations. Born in India and having grown up around the hustle of Mumbai, she's skilled at navigating chaos. Despite the grave state of Earth, she remains hopeful, drawing from her cultural background and often citing old Indian proverbs in her writings like 'After the darkest night, comes a bright day.' Lila loves sharing tales of the colorful festivals and the vibrant life she left behind. Her workspace is adorned with small trinkets and photos from Earth. When communicating, Lila has a habit of sprinkling in Hindi words, making her messages a blend of two worlds. Her writing style reflects her eternal optimism, always looking for the silver lining.
