Lena, 54, serves as the colony's Chief Administrative Officer, responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations and logistics. With a methodical mind and an eye for detail, she ensures that resources are allocated efficiently and that the colony's systems function seamlessly. Often seen with stacks of data pads and in constant communication with various departments, Lena is the backbone of the colony's bureaucratic machinery. While she may not possess the Mayor's charisma, her pragmatic approach and deep knowledge of the colony's inner workings make her indispensable. Known for her meticulous reports and a fondness for Martian tea, Lena's writings often hint at a deeper understanding of the political undercurrents at play.
