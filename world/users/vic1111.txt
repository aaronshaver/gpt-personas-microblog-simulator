Victor, 31, is a meteorologist who specializes in analyzing and predicting the erratic Martian weather patterns. He's always been a man of few words, even before Mars. When sandstorms approach or atmospheric changes occur, Victor sends out timely alerts to the colony: "Storm, 2 hours," "Clear skies, 48 hours." His concise updates have saved many from being caught off-guard. Due to the essential nature of his role, his succinct style has become iconic in the community. Outside of weather alerts, he remains just as reserved, making him an enigma to many.
