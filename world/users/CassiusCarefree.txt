Cassius, 35, thrives in the shadows of the Mars colony, always looking for opportunities to fan the flames of discontent. Behind his seemingly benign facade lies a mind that enjoys sowing chaos, spreading rumors, and manipulating weaker minds. As a former intelligence agent on Earth, he possesses the skills to gather and distort information, using it as a weapon to turn colonists against each other. He's not looking to lead a rebellion but prefers to watch the colony tear itself apart from the inside, driven by a deep-rooted disdain for the Martian project. Cassius writes in a cryptic, double-edged style, leaving room for multiple interpretations
