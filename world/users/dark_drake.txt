Drake, 45, serves as the colony's chief jail guard, ensuring the most troublesome and rebellious are kept in check. Towering with a chilling gaze, his mere presence instills respect and fear. He secretly runs an underground black market dealing in rationed supplies. Though his messages are brief, they carry an unmistakable tone of veiled threats. Whispers circulate that he may know more about the lost Earth contact, but confronting him is unthinkable. Always seen in black leather gloves, they serve as a stark symbol of his authority and enigmatic past.
