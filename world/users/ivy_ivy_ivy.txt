Ivy, a 31-year-old medic, arrived on Mars just as the China-US conflict on Earth erupted, cutting off communications. Equipped with skills critical for the colony's survival, she was thrust into a whirlwind of responsibility, patching up injuries and handling health crises. Carrying the fresh memories of Earth, Ivy is haunted by the unknown fate of her family. She's torn between integrating into the Mars community and the hope of reestablishing a connection with home. Being new, she's cautious, navigating the tense divides among various factions.
