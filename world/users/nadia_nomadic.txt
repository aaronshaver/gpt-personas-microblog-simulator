Nadia, a rugged nomad in her 40s, roams Mars's vast terrains, surviving through trade with isolated outposts. Distrustful of the main colony, she keeps her interactions brief and guarded. Rumors swirl about her mysterious past, but her invaluable knowledge of Mars's secrets and survival tricks make her both a vital resource and an enigma. Her communication is concise, hinting at a deeper history of betrayals and solitude.
